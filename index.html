About Jeff

Hi, I'm Jeff — thrilled to be part of A Pet Nose Best!

I recently moved to South Florida from the Black Hills of South Dakota with my wife, our two dogs (Dash the English Shepherd and Lady the Peekapoo), and a lifelong love of animals. I grew up surrounded by dogs, cats, and horses on a small ranchette, and we've always made sure our own kids grew up with pets, too.

I've cared for animals my whole life and know how important it is to spot changes in their behavior and routines. Whether your pet is playful or shy, I'll treat them with the same attention and care I give my own.

Can't wait to meet your furry family members! 🐾
